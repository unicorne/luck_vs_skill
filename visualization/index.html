Insights

Why These Differences Exist

The Selection Filter

Basketball rewards extreme height, shrinking the talent pool. With fewer viable candidates, the skill gap between teams narrows — making the NBA the most skill-driven league.

Sample Size Matters

The NBA plays 82 games per season while the NFL plays only 17. Fewer games mean less opportunity for skill to separate from noise — raising the NFL's luck contribution.

Money Talks

Leagues without strict salary caps (like European football) let wealthy clubs hoard talent. This amplifies skill differences — lowering apparent luck in leagues like the Premier League.

eSports: Digital Meritocracy

LoL (23% luck) is more skill-driven than MLB or La Liga. Structured league formats and best-of series help the best teams rise. Dota 2's higher variance (34%) reflects its comeback mechanics.
